Accessibility Statement

Bothot47.com is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We strive to continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

  • All images include descriptive alt text.
  • Navigation is accessible via keyboard.
  • We use high-contrast colors for readability.
  • Text can be resized using browser controls.
  • If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us and we will address your concerns promptly.

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our site and are committed to making improvements.
